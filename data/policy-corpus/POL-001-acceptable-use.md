# Acceptable Use Policy

- Policy ID: POL-001
- Owner: Workplace Technology
- Classification: Internal (synthetic)
- Version: 2.0
- Effective: 2026-01-15

This document belongs to the fictional Northstar Demo Organization and contains no real business data.

## 1. Approved use

Organization systems are for authorized learning, administrative, and collaboration activities. Limited personal use is permitted when it is lawful, low volume, does not interfere with work, and does not store sensitive material.

## 2. Prohibited use

Users must not bypass access controls, share credentials, install unapproved remote-access software, run cryptocurrency mining, target external systems, or submit confidential or personal data to unapproved AI services.

## 3. Reporting

Suspected misuse must be reported to the Service Desk within one business day; urgent security events must follow POL-006.
