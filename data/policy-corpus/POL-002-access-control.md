# Access Control Policy

- Policy ID: POL-002
- Owner: Identity and Access Management
- Classification: Internal (synthetic)
- Version: 3.1
- Effective: 2026-02-01

## 1. Access principles

Access is deny-by-default, granted to named identities for a documented business task, and limited to the least privilege and shortest practical duration. Shared interactive accounts are prohibited.

## 2. Approval and review

The resource owner approves access before provisioning. Privileged grants require a second simulated approver and expire after 30 days unless renewed. All standard access is reviewed quarterly and privileged access monthly.

## 3. Removal

Access is removed within four hours of a high-risk termination notice and by the end of the next business day for routine departures or role changes.
