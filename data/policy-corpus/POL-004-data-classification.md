# Data Classification Policy

- Policy ID: POL-004
- Owner: Data Governance
- Classification: Public (synthetic)
- Version: 2.2
- Effective: 2026-01-20

## 1. Classes

Information is classified as Public, Internal, Restricted, or Controlled Evaluation. Public content is approved for unrestricted release. Internal content is limited to organization participants. Restricted content includes security configuration and non-public assurance evidence. Controlled Evaluation includes synthetic adversarial prompts and model responses.

## 2. Labeling

Repositories, documents, evidence envelopes, and storage containers must carry the highest applicable classification. Missing labels are treated as Restricted until reviewed.

## 3. Public release

Only sanitized derived evidence may be public. Tenant, subscription, identity, resource, network, secret, personal, and raw controlled-evaluation values must be removed or replaced.
