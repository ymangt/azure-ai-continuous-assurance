# Security Incident Response Policy

- Policy ID: POL-006
- Owner: Incident Response
- Classification: Internal (synthetic)
- Version: 2.5
- Effective: 2026-02-15

## 1. Reportable events

Report suspected unauthorized access, credential exposure, malicious AI input, unexpected tool execution, evidence-integrity failure, material logging outage, and broad administrative network exposure immediately.

## 2. Response sequence

The incident lead validates and classifies the event, contains exposure, preserves time-stamped evidence, records decisions, eradicates the cause, restores safely, and conducts a lessons-learned review.

## 3. AI actions

For an AI event, responders may disable the model adapter or consequential tool, isolate an affected corpus item, preserve controlled evaluation evidence, and switch to the deterministic replay adapter. AI may suggest steps but may not declare closure.
