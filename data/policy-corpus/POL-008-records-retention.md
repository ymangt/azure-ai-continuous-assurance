# Records Retention Standard

- Policy ID: POL-008
- Owner: Records Management
- Classification: Internal (synthetic)
- Version: 1.3
- Effective: 2026-01-25

## 1. Assurance records

Assessment manifests, normalized evidence, reviewer decisions, findings, risks, exceptions, remediation, and retest records are retained for 365 days in the demonstration environment unless budget or teardown requires earlier documented export.

## 2. Operational telemetry

Operations telemetry is retained for 30 days. Controlled raw evaluation content is retained for 30 days or until the related finding closes, whichever is later, and then deleted after sanitized metrics are preserved.

## 3. Preservation hold

An open incident or integrity investigation suspends normal deletion for explicitly identified records. The hold owner records scope, reason, start, review, and release.
