# Remote Work and Endpoint Policy

- Policy ID: POL-013
- Owner: Workplace Security
- Classification: Internal (synthetic)
- Version: 1.9
- Effective: 2026-01-30

## 1. Endpoint requirements

Administrative work uses a supported, encrypted, screen-locked device with current security updates and phishing-resistant MFA where available. Public or shared devices must not access privileged interfaces.

## 2. Network use

Users must not expose RDP or SSH directly to the Internet. Approved managed service interfaces must use TLS, strong authentication, least privilege, and monitoring.

## 3. Information handling

Restricted evidence must not be copied to unmanaged personal storage, messaging, or consumer AI tools.
