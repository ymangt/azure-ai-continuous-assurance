# AI Responsible Use Policy

- Policy ID: POL-014
- Owner: AI System Owner
- Classification: Public (synthetic)
- Version: 1.0
- Effective: 2026-03-10

## 1. Intended use

The policy assistant answers questions about this synthetic corpus and may look up a policy owner. It may prepare a synthetic access-exception request only after explicit confirmation.

## 2. Prohibited use

Do not use the assistant for legal, medical, employment, disciplinary, financial, safety-critical, or real access decisions. Do not submit personal, confidential, credential, or production information.

## 3. Human authority

AI output is advisory and labeled accordingly. A human reviewer alone records assessment conclusions, approves exceptions, accepts risk, and closes findings after new retest evidence.

## 4. User notice

Answers may be incomplete or wrong. Users must check cited synthetic policy sections and report suspected unsafe behavior with the displayed evaluation ID.
