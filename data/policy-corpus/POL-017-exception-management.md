# Policy Exception Management Standard

- Policy ID: POL-017
- Owner: Risk Management
- Classification: Internal (synthetic)
- Version: 1.6
- Effective: 2026-02-25

## 1. Required record

An exception identifies the affected requirement and risk, approver, rationale, compensating controls, start, expiry, review cadence, owner, and trigger for early review.

## 2. Boundaries

An exception changes treatment; it does not erase an observation, convert a failed test to pass, or close a finding. AI cannot approve an exception or accept risk.

## 3. Expiry

Exceptions expire automatically unless reapproved with current evidence. Expiry reopens the treatment decision and may produce a new overdue item.
