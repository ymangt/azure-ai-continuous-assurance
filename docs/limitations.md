# Limitations and non-claims

- This is continuous internal assurance and audit-readiness simulation, not SOC 2, ISO 27001, regulatory certification, authorization, attestation, penetration test, or independent audit.
- One project author operates simulated assessor, owner, approver, developer, and responder personas. Technical identity separation does not create human independence.
- All policies, users, tickets, incidents, prompts, attacks, and business facts are synthetic. Results do not establish suitability for real enterprise data.
- The fixed 50-case behavioral set demonstrates known behaviors and cannot prove absence of novel prompt injection, poisoning, model, or tool failures.
- Automated checks support tailored objectives. They do not prove an entire NIST control effective without scope, design, manual, and operating evidence.
- Public snapshots omit private identifiers and raw content. They demonstrate traceability shape but are not the complete audit record.
- Public service endpoints are used with authentication and compensating controls. Private endpoints, NAT Gateway, Azure Firewall, and paid Defender plans are outside the student-cost profile.
- Blob versioning and soft delete are used without locked WORM retention. Signatures and hashes are tamper-evident, not tamper-proof.
- The existing Sentinel workspace is reused and low-volume. Synthetic rule tests cannot establish production detection quality or false-positive rates.
- Foundry availability and quota are not assumed. A live model path exists only after Azure MCP quota verification and a smoke request; the deterministic replay adapter is not presented as the live recruiter demonstration.
- Point-in-time samples do not prove long-term operation. The release target requires at least 14 days and 10 successful scheduled runs; RUN-001 and RUN-002 only demonstrate the lifecycle.
- Cost figures are planning allocations, not quotes. Azure offer, region, exchange rate, free grants, retention, and usage can change.
- OSCAL files target v1.2.2 and currently pass the checksum-pinned official complete JSON schema. Schema validity does not by itself establish that the assessment judgments are sufficient or independently reviewed.
- The 18-document production corpus loader verifies its Blob manifest, byte counts, SHA-256 digests, classifications, and exact blob membership. It does not replace a human review of policy content or provenance.
